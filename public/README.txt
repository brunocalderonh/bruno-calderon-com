Drop two files into this folder (public/) and the site will pick them up automatically:

  1. headshot.jpg   — your portrait (square, ~600×600 or larger). Used in the hero.
  2. cv.pdf         — your latest CV. The "CV" link in the nav points to /cv.pdf.

To export the photo from Apple Photos:
  Photos.app → select photo → File → Export → Export Unmodified Original
  Save as headshot.jpg into this folder.

Anything you put in /public is served at the root of the site.
For example public/headshot.jpg becomes https://bruno-calderon.com/headshot.jpg.
